Sample Buyers:

Instant and cost effective access to respondents from hundreds of panel sources globally. The world’s largest and most innovative research agencies use Sample Exchange to maximize delivery, reduce labor, improve sample costs, with total flexibility and control over sample sources. Sample Exchange connects you to a broad and diverse pool of sample sources in over 40 countries and including panels, river, social media . Sample Exchange is integrated with Field Manager allowing you unrivaled control over field management with the efficiency and cost savings of Sample Exchange.

Sample Suppliers:

Fulcrum Sample Exchange is the only solution that gives you instant access to survey inventory. The world’s largest and most innovative research agencies use Fulcrum Field Manager with their entire project inventory and buy sample in a fully automated fashion. As a supplier, Sample Optimizer maximizes revenue by prioritizing your sold studies first and provides additional revenue opportunity through the Sample Exchange. Sample Exchange gives you full control to choose who can buy your sample, what topics and project types your panelist can participate in, and and what price buyers should pay.
