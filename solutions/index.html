Fulcrum Licensing

With a Fulcrum license, you are in control to field your projects your way, at your price. Direct licensing maximizes the delivery, time, and cost advantages made possible in Fulcrum. For added transparency and visibility, you will have access to full consolidated project financials and reporting dashboards. Of course, Federated Sample’s highly skilled and professional support team will always be there to help train, consult, and support your team at every step of the way.

Beyond unlocking Fulcrum’s powerful tools and features, whether you are on the buy or sell side, you will have access to several other benefits by licensing our state of the art technology:

White labeling to maintain brand image

  • Set look and feel for full brand personalization
  • Custom initial respondent invites
  • Custom intermediary messages before client entry

Dedicated Federated Sample client support

  • Around the clock, 24/7 worldwide availability
  • Free online and one-on-one training
  • Customized technical support from our client services team, FED Love Warriors, to meet your organization’s needs

Our breadth of sampling resources and sophisticated technology maximizes delivery, while offering full transparency. Fulcrum’s specialized field management tools coupled with our highly skilled project management team makes the most challenging projects look easy. So, go ahead and sign on those large-scale, global projects with confidence and let us worry about the rest. Our approach is thorough, results driven, and incorporates several primary objectives:

Enhance Deliverability.

Successfully complete the most complicated projects

  • Integrated and established relationships with suppliers of all sizes and nationalities
  • Advanced respondent screening process and continuous profiling yield successful results for even the lowest incidence and hardest-to-reach audiences

Sustain Consistency and Continuity.

Repeatable, transparent results you can rely on
