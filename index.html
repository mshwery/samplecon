Federated Sample is excited to announce the first annual SampleCon being held September 8th & 9th, 2013 in New Orleans, LA during the AMA’s Annual Marketing Conference.

SampleCon will bring together the creators, innovators and leaders who are shaping the future of market research sampling. A participant-driven event without a predefined agenda, SampleCon is a truly unique opportunity to be part of a day filled with unbridled discussion, demonstration and debate. With no boundaries, come prepared to participate as you define the content and drive the discussion.
